About Us

Elizabeth A. Carter - Chief Executive and Chief Illumination Officer.

The Dr. Is In.

In addition to being a Finance Leader in Corporate, I am a speaker, trainer, 4X bestselling author, and career coach.

As a speaker, she is inspirational, passionate, and interactive,

As a leader, she is action-oriented, empathetic, beloved and a good listener,

As a person, she is humble, resilient, thoughtful, and easy to talk to,

As a performance improvement partner, she is committed to helping young professionals and mid-level managers grow their careers and make an impact on the business.

Dr. Elizabeth Carter is an Insurance Finance and Performance Improvement Leader with extensive experience in financial analysis, performance improvement, relationship management, and talent development, for corporate and volunteer organizations. Her unique mix of financial acumen and knowledge empowerment has provided opportunities to provide meaningful performance metrics that align with strategic goals. Further, to engage leadership in discussions with facts and scenarios which influence budgeting and forecasting decisions, and empower employees with the knowledge to enhance their careers. Her brand is AAPPEAL, which is an acronym for An Absolute Passion for Performance, Engagement, Analytics, and Leadership, and it is her passion to help companies and individuals realize their full potential in these four key strategic areas.

Elizabeth's professional experience includes positions as Director of Market Segment Finance at Highmark, Inc; Director of Financial Operations at 21st Century/Farmers Insurance; Assistant Vice President of Planning and Analysis for the Agency Auto segment of American Insurance Group (AIG); multiple positions at The Hartford Insurance Company, including Finance Lead-Project Agency Access, Claims Chief Information Officer Controller, and Director of Finance. She has held positions at Allstate Insurance Company, where her insurance finance career began.

Among her many accomplishments, Elizabeth implemented an expense forecasting accuracy performance process which improved forecast accuracy at 21st Century from 66% to 85% in a 2-year period. Also at 21st Century, she developed learning and development solutions for the Finance employees to enhance their technical and leadership skills and improve the employee engagement score by 20 percentage points.

Her unique mix of financial acumen and knowledge empowerment has provided opportunities to provide meaningful performance metrics that align with strategic goals.

She was recruited to AIG to lead the development of enhancements of a premium planning model to provide more confidence and accuracy to the monthly forecasting process for the Product Management organization which resulted in an improvement of the forecast compared to actual results by 35 percentage points. During her tenure at The Hartford, Elizabeth created and facilitated a financial acumen training series to improve underwriters' knowledge of financials and how their decisions impacted profitability, and her love for training and talent development was born.

In addition to being a Distinguished Toastmaster (DTM) and Chartered Property Casualty Underwriter (CPCU), Elizabeth holds a BBA in Marketing from Hofstra University, an MBA in Management from the New York Institute of Technology, an MS and Ph.D. in Education from Capella University. She holds/has held leadership roles in Toastmasters, the CPCU Society, and the Trinidad and Tobago Cultural Association of Delaware.

Elizabeth's vision statement is that one day, there will be no gaps in businesses' and individuals' performances; everyone will have access to the information and tools to achieve her/his ultimate goals.
